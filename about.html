🏢 Our Story

We Are Ace Digitals Global

From a one-man hustle in Lagos to a full-service digital agency serving clients across Africa and beyond. This is our story.

📖 The Journey

Built in the Streets of Lagos

Ace Digitals Global started in 2018 as a solo freelance operation run by Uchenna Richard — a young digital entrepreneur from Lagos who saw a massive gap in quality digital services for African businesses.

What started as website design gigs quickly evolved into a full-stack digital agency offering websites, SEO, social media management, AI automation, and digital products — all designed specifically for the African market.

By 2022, the agency had rebranded from Ace Digitals Empire to Ace Digitals Global — reflecting a broader vision and an expanding client base spanning Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, and beyond.

Today, with 200+ websites built, 500+ businesses served, and automation systems running in 10+ countries, Ace Digitals Global is one of Africa's most results-driven digital agencies — and we're just getting started.

Uchenna Richard

Uchenna Richard is a Lagos-based digital entrepreneur, WordPress developer, content strategist, and AI automation expert who founded Ace Digitals Global in 2018 with a simple mission: help African businesses win online.

With over 7 years of hands-on experience in digital marketing, web development, and automation systems, Uchenna has become one of Nigeria's most recognized voices in the digital space — known on social media as @DigitalUche.

His approach combines deep technical expertise with strategic marketing instincts — meaning clients don't just get beautiful websites, they get digital systems that actually generate leads and revenue.
